# Eniac-expansion-to-the-Brazilian-market
Eniac, a Spain-founded online marketplace, is exploring expansion into Brazil but lacks local market knowledge and partnerships.

Magist, a Brazilian SaaS company, offers a centralized order management system to connect stores with major Brazilian marketplaces

Challenges: 

Is Magist an apropiate fit for Eniacs products ?

Are deliveries fast enough?

Thankfully, Magist has allowed Eniac to access a snapshot of their database. The data might have the answer to these concerns.
